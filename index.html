I was born and raised in Vancouver, Canada. I worked at a telco for 21 years in roles ranging from contact centre agent to quality coach, to business analyst and project manager.
Prior to my telecom career, I trained in classical piano before studying music composition at the UBC School of Music. I also taught piano, part-time for 14 years, from the age of 15 to 28.
In addition to being a VSO Seasons ticket holder since 1996, I also love traveling, running, cooking and knitting. One of my favourite pastimes is brisk stroll or run around the Stanley Park seawall.
